Growth

This was an extremely fun project to work on.

The target audience is people who play farm games, people of all age, who speak 11 languages.

The main challenges that we faced were regarding how to design a community chat system where the users dialogues would be concise, fit a small area, and be intuitive.

The project had people from many different departments giving great feedback and ideas along the way. I think this is were I personally grew the most, because I feel pretty comfortable with the technical side of things with regard to this project but for me it was more about interpersonal growth with my colleagues, as well as being firm when I had an opinion about a design or technical decision we were going to make. I know that it is a funny project but I feel like this helped me work with my colleagues and enrich cross team working at Plinga
