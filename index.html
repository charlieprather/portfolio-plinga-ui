What did I learn? Where do I begin

I have learned a plethora of knowledge working on these rich media campaigns.

I have been comfortable with HTML, CSS, and JS for a few years now but I had never extensively worked with different javascript libraries, and these projects gave me an extensive opportunity to learn and work with different libraries, especially GSAP(Greensock) and jQuery, for animations.

Working on these campaigns also provided me with the opportunity to learn Adobe Animate and Adobe After Effects, in depth. This has been huge for me, especially when it comes to prototyping animations for other projects regarding Web design/UI/UX. I also learned how to sync up videos with GSAP animations; marrying After Effects and javascript was a really cool tool I picked up working on this projects.

The soft skills I enhanced while working on these campaigns were also invaluable. These campaigns are orchestrated with people all over the world, so working with people in various languages and understanding their perspective has been a cultivated skill I have been working on as well. Also the business/marketing element of these endeavors has been a great learning experience for me. I have been learning different marketing jargon and tips regarding timing and how to entice potential customers, despite my graphic design background.

After working on many campaigns I have created templates and different systems at the company in order to speed up our work process. I am also currently developing video tutorials for people wanted to learn more about rich media and how it works.
