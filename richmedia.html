Rich Media for Yoki Network

The situation

I often work on advertisements for major companies for Plinga's partner company, Yoki Network. I am responsible for designing and coding advertisements based off of a particular campaign. I have been doing this for almost two years and I really love the work because it is extremely diverse and very playful. I have learned a lot about different js libraries while doing this. I have found it interesting in learning that there is a huge marketing/sales element that is often guides the design.
